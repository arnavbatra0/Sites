MEMORANDUM

TO: The Interested Reader FROM: Arnav Batra, Someone Who Notices Things DATE: Estb. June, 2026 SUBJECT: Who is "Briefly, Arnav"?

I'm Arnav — eighteen, finishing school, about to start law school, and apparently someone who can't stop noticing things. I grew up in a tier-2 city, spent too much time on the internet, built things that didn't always work, and somewhere along the way developed a habit of asking why something is the way it is instead of just accepting it.

Some people asked me why I'm writing this. "It doesn't solve any problem," they said. My answer was simple — this is not a business. I just want to write. Briefly, Arnav exists because I observe things that don't add up, and writing is how I make sense of them.

*Taking legal advice exclusively from Saul Goodman. ⚖️

I'm not here to give you answers. I'm here to ask questions out loud, in public, where you can watch. If you walk away wanting to question something you'd normally just accept — a rule, a system, an assumption — then this page did its job.
